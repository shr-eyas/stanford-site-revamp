# Research

Our research focuses on developing intelligent robotic systems that can learn, adapt, and collaborate with humans in complex real-world environments.

## Areas

### Learning from Demonstration
image: https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1485827404703-89b55fcc595e?w=600&h=400&fit=crop
Learning manipulation skills from human demonstrations using interactive imitation learning and dynamical systems.

### Optimal Control
image: https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1518314916381-77a37c2a49ae?w=600&h=400&fit=crop
Developing adaptive optimal control methods for uncertain robotic systems with real-world applications.

### Human-Robot Interaction
image: https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1531746790731-6c087fecd65a?w=600&h=400&fit=crop
Creating safe and intuitive interfaces for human-robot collaboration in manufacturing and service applications.
