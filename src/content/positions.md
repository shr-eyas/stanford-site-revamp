# Positions

We are always looking for motivated researchers to join our team at the Human-interactive Robotics Lab.

## Open Positions

### Ph.D. Students

We are looking for Ph.D. students with strong backgrounds in:

- Robotics and Control Systems
- Machine Learning and Deep Learning
- Computer Vision
- Human-Robot Interaction

**Requirements:**
- Master's degree in Robotics, Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, or related field
- Strong programming skills (Python, C++)
- Experience with ROS is a plus
- Good communication skills in English

### Master's Students

We offer research opportunities for Master's students interested in:

- Learning from Demonstration
- Optimal Control for Manipulation
- Vision-based Robot Control
- Safe Human-Robot Collaboration

### Research Associates

Positions for Research Associates are available for candidates with:

- Ph.D. in Robotics or related field
- Strong publication record
- Experience in experimental robotics

## How to Apply

Interested candidates should send the following to **ravipr[at]iisc[dot]ac[dot]in**:

1. CV with list of publications
2. Statement of research interests (1-2 pages)
3. Contact information for 2-3 references
4. Transcripts (for student positions)

Please use the subject line: **[Position Type] Application - Your Name**
